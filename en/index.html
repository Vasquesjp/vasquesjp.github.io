NLW WORLD CUP 2022

Project built during the Next Level Week event by Rocketseat, using the technologies HTML5, CSS3, and JS. It is a calendar of the Brazil games in the 2022 World Cup group stage. JS was used for DOM manipulation and I implemented a color selector for the theme of the page.

TIME NOW

Project built during the javascript course on the Curso em Vídeo platform, using HTML5, CSS3, and JS technologies. The page obtains the local time, extracts and formats it using javascript, and displays it on the screen along with an image and background that change according to the time of day (morning, afternoon, or night).

Hello, my name is João Paulo Vasques, I am years old and currently I am studying front-end programming. I have always been fascinated by how things work and this curiosity has led me to follow a career in technology. My passion for technology began in my childhood when I managed my own MMORPG server using PHP and MySQL. I spent my adolescence programming games on the Game Maker engine.

When I was 19, I became a father and I had to take other paths, because programming for me was only a hobby. I went through the areas of construction (2 periods of Technical in Buildings), electricity (Technical in Electrical Engineering, where my favorite subjects involved Ladder programming and Arduino) and I worked in the telephony area (PABX programming).

In 2022, I decided that being in the technology area is what I want for my future and I fell in love with front-end development, I already had some knowledge of HTML and CSS due to having kept several websites and blogs to promote my games and I am deepening more and more in the sea that is Javascript. I am excited to continue my journey in the field of technology and see where it takes me.
